Anthony is an independent interface designer (senior UX/UI designer). In 2013, he co-founded Collectif Bam, a global design studio committed to societal transitions. Ten years later, the agency evolved into Praticable, a cooperative focused on responsible digital design. Since early 2024, Anthony has been working independently on digital projects of public interest and teaching his profession at various schools and universities.
