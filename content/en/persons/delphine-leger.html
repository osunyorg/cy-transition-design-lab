This thesis aims to examine the design practices implemented in organizational transition processes. We aim to identify how these practices fit into complex systems. We assume that organizational transitions towards sustainability depend on their ability to visualize and archive the systems in which they are rooted and towards which they strive.

Using the design transition methodology, we use field projects to study how design tools can help employees of a big firm to transition towards a more sustainable system.
