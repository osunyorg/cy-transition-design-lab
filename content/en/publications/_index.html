We produce four main types of contributions: 

1- empirical : we analyze design and decision-making cultures of actors in transition

2- corpus oriented: we analyze the semio-technical characteristics and properties of media for design, mediation and decision-making.

3- experimental:  we prototype, test, and evaluate alternative solutions that meet the needs of stakeholders in relation to their cultures.

4- theorerical: we bring in media studies to consolidate design theory.
